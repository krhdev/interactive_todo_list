Help & Guide

Where is my data saved?

Your lists and tasks are saved on this device only, inside your browser — unless you choose to sign in, in which case they're saved securely to your account instead. See "Signing in and syncing across devices" below.

If you're not signed in, this means:

  • Your data on your phone is separate from your data on your laptop.
  • If you open the app in a different browser on the same device, your lists won't be there.
  • If you clear your browser's site data or cache, your lists will be gone.
Tip: Export your lists regularly as a backup, especially before clearing your browser or switching devices.
Signing in and syncing across devices

By default your lists are stored only on this device. If you'd like your lists available on your phone, laptop, and any other device, you can create an account and sign in.

  • On opening the app, you'll see a sign-in card. Choose Sign Up if you're new, or Sign In if you already have an account.
  • Enter your email and a password, then confirm your email if signing up for the first time.
  • Once signed in, your lists and tasks are saved to your account and will appear on any device you sign into.
  • Don't want to sign in? Click Skip to keep using the app with on-device storage only.
  • To sign out, use the Sign out button that appears in the sidebar next to your email.
Note: Local (device-only) data and cloud (signed-in) data are kept separate. Switching between them won't merge your lists automatically — export first if you want to combine them.
How do I use lists?

Lists let you group tasks together — for example, Work, Shopping, or a project name.

  • Type a name in the New List box and press Create List or hit Enter.
  • Click any list tab to switch to it and see its tasks.
  • Tasks belong to the list they were added to — switching lists shows only that list's tasks.
  • To remove a list, click the ✕ on its tab. This deletes the list and all its tasks permanently.
How do categories work?

Categories let you group your lists together — for example, Work, Personal, Daily, or Weekly.

  • When creating a new list, choose a category from the dropdown, or select + New Category to type your own.
  • Use the category pills above your lists to filter which lists are shown. Click All to see everything.
  • Each pill shows a count of how many lists are in that category.
Tip: Lists in the Daily or Weekly categories get special treatment — see the next section.
Daily and Weekly list auto-reset

If you set a list's category to Daily or Weekly, completed tasks in that list will automatically be marked as active again — no need to manually untick them.

  • Daily lists reset once every day, the first time you open the app that day.
  • Weekly lists reset every Monday.
  • Resets are recorded in Recent Changes so you can see what happened.
Tip: Great for routines — a "Daily" list for habits, or a "Weekly" list for chores.
What is the Focus Task of the Day?

The Focus Task card highlights one active task at a time, to help you concentrate on a single thing.

  • A task is picked automatically from your active tasks.
  • Click Done to complete it and clear the card, or the re-roll button to pick a different task.
  • Use the clear button to dismiss the card without completing anything.
  • If you have no active tasks, the card hides itself automatically.
How do I manage tasks?
  • Add a task: Select a list, type in the Add a Task box, and press Add Task or Enter.
  • Complete a task: Tick the checkbox. It moves to Completed Tasks.
  • Reopen a task: In Completed Tasks, untick the checkbox to move it back to active.
  • Edit a task: Click Edit, make your change, then Save (or press Enter). Press Cancel or Escape to discard.
  • Delete a task: Click Delete. It moves to Deleted Tasks — it's not gone yet.
  • Restore a task: In Deleted Tasks, click Restore to bring it back to active.
  • Permanently remove deleted tasks: In Deleted Tasks, use the Clear Deleted button. This cannot be undone.
How do I move a task to a different list?

There are two ways to move a task from one list to another:

  • Move button: Click the → button on a task, then choose the destination list from the dropdown.
  • Drag and drop: Drag a task and drop it onto a different list tab to move it there directly.
How do I back up my lists? (Export)

Exporting saves all your lists and tasks into a single file on your device that you can keep as a backup or move to another device.

  • In the sidebar, click ⬇ Export Lists.
  • A file named something like krhdev-todo-backup-2026-06-22.json will download automatically.
  • Save it somewhere safe — your Downloads folder, Google Drive, email it to yourself, or copy it to another device.
Tip: Get into the habit of exporting once a week, or any time you've done a lot of work in the app.
How do I restore or move my lists? (Import)

Importing reads a backup file and loads your lists back into the app. You'd use this after clearing your browser, switching to a new device, or restoring from a backup.

  • In the sidebar, click ⬆ Import Lists and pick your backup file.
  • You'll be asked to choose between two options:
  • Merge — adds the lists from the backup file alongside any lists already on this device. Use this when moving to a new device that already has some lists, or when combining two sets of data.
  • Replace — wipes everything currently in the app and restores exactly what's in the backup file. Use this when restoring a backup from scratch.
Tip: If you're unsure which to pick, choose Merge — it's the safer option as it won't delete anything you already have.
How do I use this on my phone or install it?

The app works in any mobile browser without installing anything. For a better experience, you can add it to your home screen so it opens like a normal app:

  • Android (Chrome): Tap the three-dot menu and choose Add to Home Screen, or look for an install icon in the address bar.
  • iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow), then Add to Home Screen.
  • Desktop (Chrome or Edge): Look for an install icon at the right end of the address bar.

Once installed, the app opens in its own window without the browser around it, and works offline.

Note: Installing the app on your phone does not sync data with any other device unless you're signed in. Your lists are still stored on that device only when using local storage.
Light and dark mode

You can switch between light and dark mode in Settings. Your choice is saved and will be remembered next time you open the app.

Can I personalise the app with my name?

Yes. Go to Settings and find the Personalisation section. Enter your name and press Save — the sidebar title and mobile header will update to show your name instead of the default.

  • Your name is saved in your browser, so it persists between visits.
  • It's stored on this device only — it won't appear on other devices unless you set it there too.
  • To go back to the default, use the Reset to Default button in the same section.
How do I clear all my data?

Go to Settings and use the Clear All Data button. This permanently deletes all lists, tasks, and history from this device.

Tip: Export your lists before clearing data if you want to keep them.
