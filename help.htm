Help & Guide

Where is my data saved?

If you are signed in, your lists and tasks are saved securely in the cloud and will sync across all your devices automatically.

If you use the app without signing in, your data is saved on this device only, inside your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.

  • Your data on your phone is separate from your data on your laptop unless you sign in.
  • If you clear your browser's site data or cache, local data will be gone.
Tip: Sign in to keep your lists safe and accessible everywhere. Export regularly as an extra backup.
Signing in and accounts

When you open the app you'll see a sign in card. You can sign in, create an account, or continue without signing in.

  • Sign up: Enter your email and a password and click Create Account. Check your email for a confirmation link.
  • Sign in: Enter your email and password and click Sign In.
  • Continue without signing in: Uses local storage on this device only.
  • Sign out: Click Sign out next to your email in the sidebar.
Tip: Once signed in, your lists load automatically on any device.
How do I use lists and categories?

Lists let you group tasks together. Each list belongs to a category which helps you organise and filter them.

  • Type a name in the New List box, choose a category from the dropdown, and press Create List or Enter.
  • To add a new category, select ＋ New category... from the dropdown, type a name and press Enter or click Add.
  • Click any list tab to switch to it and see its tasks.
  • To remove a list, click the ✕ on its tab. This deletes the list and all its tasks permanently.

Available categories include General, Today, Daily, Weekly, Training, Research, and Personal Projects. Daily and Weekly lists reset automatically — see the section below.

Filtering lists by category

Above your lists you'll see category pills — buttons that filter which lists are shown.

  • Click a category pill to show only lists in that category.
  • Click All to see every list at once.
  • Click All again to toggle back to category view.

Each pill shows a count of how many lists are in that category. Each list tab shows a count of active tasks.

Daily and Weekly auto-reset

Lists in the Daily category automatically un-tick all completed tasks every day at midnight. Lists in the Weekly category reset every Monday.

  • Use Daily for recurring habits or routines — take medication, morning checklist, etc.
  • Use Weekly for things you do once a week.
  • Tasks are un-ticked, not deleted — they come back as active ready to do again.
Tip: Resets are logged in Recent Changes so you can see when they happened.
How do I manage tasks?
  • Add a task: Select a list, type in the Add a Task box, and press Add Task or Enter.
  • Complete a task: Tick the checkbox. It moves to Completed Tasks.
  • Reopen a task: In Completed Tasks, untick the checkbox to move it back to active.
  • Edit a task: Click Edit, make your change, then Save or press Enter. Press Cancel or Escape to discard.
  • Delete a task: Click Delete. It moves to Deleted Tasks — not gone yet.
  • Restore a task: In Deleted Tasks, click Restore to bring it back.
  • Permanently remove deleted tasks: Use the Clear Deleted button. This cannot be undone.
  • Move a task: Click the → button on a task and pick a list from the dropdown.
Subtasks

Any task can have subtasks — smaller steps that sit underneath it.

  • Click + Add subtask under any active task to add one.
  • Type the subtask name and press Enter or click Add.
  • Tick a subtask checkbox to mark it done.
  • Click ✕ on a subtask to remove it permanently.
  • An orange 2/3 badge on the parent task shows how many subtasks are done.
Moving tasks between lists

You can move any active task to a different list in two ways:

  • Move button: Click the → button on a task, then pick the destination list from the dropdown.
  • Drag and drop (desktop only): Drag a task and drop it onto a list tab. The tab highlights in orange when you hover over it.
Note: Drag and drop is not available on iOS — use the → button instead.
Focus Task of the Day

The Focus Task card highlights one task for you to concentrate on. It picks from tasks in your currently active category.

  • ✓ Done: Marks the task complete and picks a new one.
  • ↺ Re-roll: Picks a different task at random.
  • Clear focus: Dismisses the current focus without completing the task.
  • ✕ (top right): Hides the focus card entirely. A ⚡ Show Focus Task button appears in its place to bring it back.
Tip: Switch category pill to get a focus task from a specific category only.
How do I back up my lists? (Export)

Exporting saves all your lists and tasks into a file you can keep as a backup or move to another device.

  • In the sidebar, click ⬇ Export Lists.
  • A file named something like krhdev-todo-backup-2026-06-22.json will download automatically.
  • Save it somewhere safe — Downloads, Google Drive, or email it to yourself.
Tip: Export regularly, especially before clearing your browser or switching devices.
How do I restore or move my lists? (Import)

Importing reads a backup file and loads your lists back into the app.

  • In the sidebar, click ⬆ Import Lists and pick your backup file.
  • Merge — adds lists from the file alongside any existing lists.
  • Replace — wipes everything and restores exactly what's in the backup.
Tip: If unsure, choose Merge — it's the safer option.
How do I use this on my phone or install it?

The app works in any mobile browser. For a better experience, add it to your home screen:

  • iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share button ⬆ then Add to Home Screen.
  • Android (Chrome): Tap the three-dot menu and choose Add to Home Screen, or look for an install icon in the address bar.
  • Desktop (Chrome or Edge): Look for an install icon at the right end of the address bar.

Once installed, the app opens without the browser around it and works offline.

Personalising the app

Go to Settings to personalise the app:

  • Your name: Enter your name in the Personalisation section and the app title updates everywhere.
  • Reset to default: Click Reset to Default to go back to KRHDev.
  • Theme: Switch between light and dark mode. Your choice is saved automatically.
How do I clear all my data?

Go to Settings and use the Clear All Data button. This permanently deletes all lists, tasks, and history from this device.

Tip: Export your lists before clearing data if you want to keep them.
